## 🔵 SECTION 8: FAQ

### İngilizce

**Q: Will YouTube ban my channel for using AI replies?**

A: No. Creavidy operates within YouTube's guidelines. Every video is creator-approved (human-in-the-loop), properly disclosed as AI-assisted, and sent to *your own members* as a perk — not spam. YouTube's own voice reply feature shows they encourage personalized member engagement.

**Q: How realistic are the AI videos?**

A: Our videos use your real face and cloned voice. They're short (5-15 seconds), focused, and personal. Think of them as video voice-notes, not Hollywood productions. Members love the personal touch.

**Q: How much time does this save me?**

A: Creators report saving 20+ hours per month. The Tinder-style approval takes about 5 minutes per day. Your AI twin handles the rest.

**Q: Can members tell it's AI?**

A: We include a small "AI-assisted" disclosure as required. But members appreciate it — they know you can't personally video-reply to thousands of people. What matters is the personal touch and the effort to make them feel special.

**Q: What if the AI says something wrong?**

A: Nothing goes out without your approval. Every video sits in your review queue. Swipe right to send, left to skip, or tap to edit the script. You have full control.
